General Settings

Enable Automatic Downloads When enabled, images meeting your criteria will be downloaded automatically as you browse.

Download Criteria

Images will only be downloaded if their width OR height is greater than or equal to this value. This helps filter out small icons, thumbnails, and UI elements.
Tip: Set to 600px to capture most content images while ignoring avatars and icons. Set to 1000px or higher for high-resolution images only.

Save Location

Images will be saved to: Downloads/{your-folder-name}/
Files are named sequentially: img-1.png, img-2.jpg, etc.
Note: Invalid characters (< > : " | ? *) will be automatically removed from the folder name.
